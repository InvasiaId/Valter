Hi I'm Minami Hamabe

August 29, 2000

Hello, I'm Minami Hamabe (浜辺 美波). I'm a Japanese actress born in Ishikawa Prefecture. I became widely known after starring in the movie "Let Me Eat Your Pancreas" (2017) as Sakura Yamauchi. In addition to movies, I'm also active in various television dramas and commercial projects.
